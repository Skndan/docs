The salary template simplifies the assignment of salary components to employees, enabling easy configuration for each role. When creating a new employee, associate the appropriate salary template and adjust values as needed, including changes to the Cost to Company (CTC).

### Form

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/payroll/salary-template-form-2.png"/>
</Frame>  
When adding salary components, you can view them categorized by component type. Modify values or remove components as necessary. At the bottom of the table, the Cost to Company (CTC) is highlighted. Any remaining amount will automatically be added to the fixed allowance. Additionally, every salary template will include the Fixed Allowance, regardless of roles or designations.
<Info>
  Fixed allowance is a residual component of salary that is left after allocations are made for all other components.
</Info>
